FAQs

  • 01. Why are all images blurry in the download package?

    The images included in the download package are for demonstration purposes only. For high-resolution versions, please refer to the sources listed in the Credits section.

  • 02. Why might some videos not work?

    Certain videos may function only on a live server. To ensure all features work correctly, please upload your website to a real server.

  • 03. How do I generate a support ticket?
    • Go to our support portal.
    • Log in with your account details.
    • Click on "Submit a Ticket" and fill out the form with your issue or question.
    • Submit the ticket, and you will receive a confirmation email with a ticket number.
